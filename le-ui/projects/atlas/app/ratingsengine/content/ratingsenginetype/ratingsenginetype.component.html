What do you want to predict?

  • Customers That Will Purchase A Product For The First Time

    Build a model to sell a Product Bundle to the customers that haven’t bought that Bundle before. Use this to sell durable products solution extensions, or to break into new customer cohorts with consumable goods or renewable licenses. If some customers in the Segment have already bought it, they won’t get Rated.

  • Customers That Will Purchase Again Next {{vm.periodType}}

    Build a model to sell a Product Bundle to the customers that did buy the Product Bundle in the past, but now haven’t refilled within the expected timeframe. Use this to win back customers that may be refilling elsewhere, or to be proactive with a renewal or upgrade conversation. If some customers in the Segment have never bought, or have refilled too recently, they won’t get Rated.
  • Rate a Segment Based On Specific Rules

    Build a model by defining exact Rating logic. Use purchase information and campaign behavior to target customers with product fit and readiness. You can also layer Ratings from other types of Models with additional logic, for example, combine customer activity with business fit ratings. All Segment records will get Rated.
  • A Custom Event (Upload a Training File)

    Build a model trained on a file with your successes marked as a flag column. You can build an Account Fit training set by exporting a Segment you want to score, adding your best Fit Accounts from the past, and loading into Atlas for machine learning. All Segment records will get Rated.
