Account IDs

You chose to use your ID field for deduplication. It is important the IDs associated with any previously uploaded accounts remain the same, and any new accounts that you upload have a unique ID.

Note: This is a one time choice because changing later will cause your data to become unmanagable. If you feel you need to change it, please contact Lattice Support

Use Your Unique IDs for Deduplication
If you have unique identifiers for each record, select this option. It is important that every account has a unique ID, and that they do not change on subsequent uploads.
Use Lattice Matching Algorithms to Manage Deduplication
If you would rather have Lattice manage unique identifiers, select this option. This uses our matching and deuplication algorithms. The advantage is you don't have to worry about managing IDs and making sure they don't change as you upload more data later). The drawback is the matching has a possibility of error.
